Cumulative percentage coverage


Cumulative percentage coverage across the whole project area including Devon and Somerset.
The percentage coverage is proportional to the total number of premises required to reach 90% superfast coverage by the end of 2016, which is itself proportional to the total number of premises as they stood back in 2012 and dependent on delivery of private providers 2012 rollout plans.

NOTE that the grey shaded section represents a simple projection from the current percentage coverage to the 90% end-of-project goal; however the line itself does not represent target figures or milestones.

Source: Devon County Council Economy Team
